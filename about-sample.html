Established in the year 1992, Three Aar is engaged in the business of manufacturing filtration products and providing customized filtration solutions to a wide gamut of industries such us, Automobile, Food and Beverages, Chemical, Medical, Electronics, Paint Shops, Agriculture etc.



About Group



Three Aar, a flagship of Dwarkason Group of Companies, and its associate companies M/s Filters & Filter Fabrics and M/S Saraswati Corporation, aim to provide world-class products to cater to the requirements of customers. Qualified technocrats fuel the functioning of the group. These engineers give momentum to Three Aar in meeting the standardized as well as customized industrial requirements of its esteemed customers. In addition to having own infrastructure and facilities, Three Aar has strategic tie-ups across the globe to provide state of the art filter products to its customers.
